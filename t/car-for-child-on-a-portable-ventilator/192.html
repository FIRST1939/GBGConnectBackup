Car for Child on a Portable Ventilator

Technical Discussion
Doug May 17, 2018, 7:00pm 1

A SpongeBob was a perfect fit for this child, but the child uses a portable ventilator and is sun-sensitive. We mounted a tray on the front of the car so the ventilator can ride along, and added a cute canopy. Now the child will have true independent mobility. The rear attachments (guide post for parent-assist; rear head brace; frame for canopy) are built as a separate sub-assembly and attach to the body at three points using only six bolts.
