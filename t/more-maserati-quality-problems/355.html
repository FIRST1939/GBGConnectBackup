More Maserati Quality Problems

Technical Discussion
Doug March 9, 2019, 2:59pm 1

We have had quality problems with Maseratis before, but this one really got my attention. The car was brand new, manufacture date 9/18. Apparently the black battery wire had been accidentally cut. It was glued back together with thermal glue (not even twisted) and then wrapped with masking tape. See attached pictures. Apparently they “tested” their “fix” because the shipping protectors on the battery terminals were not installed, and I found one in the bottom of the car. Indeed the car operated, but for how long? And this repair was clearly not safe. There was also a screw missing from the relay box, but that is less of a problem. My only suggestion is to inspect any Maseratis carefully, especially the wires, connectors, and components that carry higher current.
